Core Laboratories & Fields

1. Embedded AI & Network Quantization (TinyML)

Developing techniques to compress large Convolutional Neural Networks and speech recognition pipelines. Focusing on fixed-point arithmetic optimization, INT8 and INT4 post-training quantization, and kernel compiling for ARM Cortex-M microcontrollers to enable high-accuracy voice triggers on edge hardware.

2. Decentralized Distributed Swarm Computing

Investigating client-side parallel computing structures. Building WebSocket bridging clients that allow lightweight developer extensions to split and distribute heavy Large Language Model (LLM) processing logic across CPU and GPU pools of local devices in unified compute swarms.

3. Biological Signal Processing (EMG & ECG)

Designing analog sensor interfaces and embedded filtering loops for biological signals. Extracting real-time electromyography (EMG) muscle envelope thresholds, and sensor-fusion algorithms that calibrate personalized baselines to detect physiological stress spikes.

4. Laboratory Prototyping Workshop

Operating a custom-built home mechanical and chemical workshop. Researching Marlin firmware movement optimizations on customized 3D printers, and electrochemistry plating processes to build multi-layer through-hole PCBs at home.
